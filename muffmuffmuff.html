MuffMuffMuff – Modeler

MuffMuffMuff, also known as Muff, is a skilled 3D modeler at Deadcode Unit who specializes in creating detailed models using Blender. Muff designs characters, environments, and assets that enhance the quality of the team’s projects. His work plays a vital role of every game or software Deadcode Unit develops.
